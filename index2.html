Podcast : EDGE OF TEENAGE

I am a Programmer

feat. Aman Malhotra

On this episode of EDGE OF TEENAGE, we get into a deep conversation with Aman, who is a programmer and knows several coding languages. 19 years old, he is currently working on maintaining a strong hold in machine learning and artificial intelligence topics. Having been an avid reader, he gives an insight into his life before and after JEE . We discuss about different perspectives of teenage life and dive deeper into the processes followed by the Indian Education system to bifurcate students on the basis of marks.

I am a Bodybuilder

feat. Guneet Talwar

From Overweight to SHREDDED !!! An 18 year old engineering student making strides in the field of fitness as he completes about 3 years of working out and always guiding his friends who are just starting out in the world of lifting weights, talks about his experience of bodybuilding. He has always been a fan of fitness as is seen from his past in sports and calisthenics. He drops some truth bombs as he encounters with myths of gym and working out. Giving clear cut clarifications about techniques and strategies for those who want to start out in the line of building up their bodies, he goes on to tell us some of his interests and those amazing stories. Listen to this podcast if you want to be fit and healthy if not shredded.

I am a Student Journalist

feat. Ankita Sharma

Gave the JEE Exam but joined Journalism !! Listen to this podcast to hear the wonderful story of Ankita Sharma, a 19 year old girl who went against the societal norms and dared to live her passion. Interestingly, her life is full of turnarounds but she still aspire to dream and become what she really wanted. Always been an A grader, she has also participated in sports competition and has brought several laurels in her name. A student of Journalism and Mass Communication, she tells us about the scenario of Mass media in India and abroad after which she describes the best spirit of journalism students. Full of enthusiasm, she goes onto sharing her Global Volunteer experience in Egypt and How she took the interviews of some of the most famous women personalities of our time.

I am a Zumba Instructor

feat. Harshneet Kaur

The youngest Certified ZUMBA Instructor, Harshneet Kaur , is with us on this episode to share her journey of following her passion and making a living out of it. She started choreography at an age when common kids couldn't even spell it. Remember , How we used to see some stylish dancing style in some hollywood movie and would shout out,'' That's salsa dance, I want to learn it!!'', Well! She is doing it now and over the past few years she has done various choreography gig for her Choreography house and is simply just unstoppable. Through the episode, she takes us through her 16 years of dancing journey and tells us what benefits Dance has for our physical and mental health. Harshneet also talks about the issues she had to face while making a very difficult decision which eventually changed her life.

I am a Law Student : Part 1

feat. Lovanshi Arora

On this first part of the fifth episode of the podcast, We get to know about Lovanshi and how she entered the legal world. We discuss about How life changes during the transition from school to college and What new experiences this transition takes us through. Lawyers are considered to be some of the most smart people and through this podcast we get to know the mentality and back stage lives of a lawyer in the making. We talk about passion and peer pressure in detail and how it is difficult for teenagers to choose the correct career. And as we progress to the end of the first part we have already started the discussion on some major issues which hold national importance and as a matter of fact need to be discussed by teenagers.

I am a Law Student : Part 2

feat. Lovanshi Arora

This is in continuation with the previous part of the same episode. Lovanshi continued to shed light on issues of great importance in today's world. We start with LGBTQ+ community following with Women Empowerment then sliding into the topic of Climate Change after which we settle at Mental Health. These issues which are of such significance in the contemporary world are thoroughly explained by the guest in a very simplistic and cool manner. I say cool because teenagers tend to adapt things which they seem are cool and thus it hints that we need to make it cool to discuss these topics among our families and friends.

I am a Designer

feat. Abhay Chopra

From getting rejected several times by the interviewers to being busy with several internships and personal projects, this Delhi lad turned his life upside down in just a year when he stopped crazily running after a perfect life and started making this one perfect. He is UI/UX Designer and a coder and a content creator and a research paper publisher and I'm officially out of 'and' s . He has been a president of school societies and have done sport part too. From being a computer geek to a youtuber to an almost international student to a social worker , this guy has done it all. Tune it to listen to the life story of Abhay Chopra on this episode of EDGE OF TEENAGE podcast.

I am a Psychology Student

feat. Nidhi

On this episode of EDGE OF TEENAGE, we have a beautiful and strong soul Nidhi. She has gone through a lot and has come out braver and passionate as ever. She is persistently doing the efforts to spread awareness about issues such as Mental health, Suicide Prevention, Sexual Abuse and Domestic Violence. Now, a psychology student, she is the perfect person to tell you to be aware about yourself and take care of loved ones. On this beautiful planet, all are not angels. We need to do the effort to help those in need and the least expected from everyone is the action of not judging those who have been a victim of these activities. Let's also end the stigma around mental health and talk openly about it. Stay Safe. Stay Healthy. Both physically and mentally.
